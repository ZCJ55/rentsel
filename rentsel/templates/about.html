About RentSel

Your Smart Rental Assistant for Selangor

Our Mission

RentSel is dedicated to providing accurate rent predictions and housing recommendations in Selangor, Malaysia. We leverage advanced machine learning algorithms and a comprehensive dataset to help international students and other tenants make informed decisions about their housing.

How It Works

Our platform uses a robust machine learning model trained on a wide range of property data, including location, size, number of rooms, and amenities. When you input your preferences, our system predicts rental values and recommends properties that best fit your criteria.

Key Features

  • Smart Rent Prediction: Get accurate monthly rent estimations for various property types in Selangor.
  • Personalized House Recommendations: Discover properties that match your specific needs and preferences.
  • History Tracking: Keep a record of your past predictions and recommendations.
  • Data-Driven Insights: Understand market trends and factors influencing rental prices.
